Homemade Lasagna

Description

A delicious homemade Italian dish consisting layers of pasta sheets, lean ground beef and yummy tomato sauce. Bon appetit!

Ingredients

  • Meat: A pound of ground beef (feel free to use pork if you want!)
  • Onion: A single diced onion.
  • Canned tomatoes: One can of canned tomatoes
  • Herbs: Two tablespoons of parsley and one crushed clove of garlic.
  • Sugar: A teaspoon of sugar to balance out the acidity of the tomatoes.
  • Spices and seasonings: A touch of dried basil, oregano, salt and black pepper.
  • Pasta sheets: Four pasta sheets.
  • Cheese: Use whatever kind of cheese you want! We recommend a combination of grated mature cheddar and shredded mozzarella.
  • Eggs: Two eggs to act as a binding agent and make the cheese layer extra creamy.

Steps

  1. Cook the meat: Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned. Add the onion and cook until translucent.
  2. Add the canned tomatoes, herbs and spices: Stir in the canned tomatoes, half the parsley, garlic, basil, oregano and 1 teaspoon of salt and sugar.
  3. Cook the pasta sheets: Boil the pasta sheets in a pan until al dente or to your liking.
  4. Make the cheese layer: Mix the grated cheddar and parmesan with the eggs and remaining parsley, salt and pepper in a bowl.
  5. Assemble the lasagna: Layer the ingredients to construct the lasagna.
  6. Bake the lasagna: Cover the lasagna with foil and bake in a preheated oven for 30 minutes. Then, remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown.
  7. Enjoy your lasagna!
