Rum Ham

Description

Ham soaked in rum. It is loaded with booze. Eating your drinks? That's genius!

Ingredients

  • Meat: 5 lbs of bone-in fully-cooked ham
  • Mustard: 1⁄3 cup Dijon mustard
  • Sugar: 3⁄4 cup brown sugar
  • Pineapple: 1 can of pineapple slices, undrained
  • Rum fluid ounces of orange-flavored rum
  • Cornstarch: 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • Water: 1⁄4 cup of water

Steps

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 150 degrees celsius (300 degrees F).
  2. Step 2: Place the ham into a roasting pan. Spread Dijon mustard over the top and sides and sprinkle with sugar. Then place the pineapple slices onto the ham, and pour 2 ounces of you orange-flavoured rum over the ham.
  3. Step 3: Bake in the preheated oven for 1-hour or until the ham is cooked through. Remove the ham and pour the remaining 2 ounces of rum over the ham.
  4. Step 4: Carefully light the rum on fire with a match. The flames will help the glaze adhere to the meat.
  5. Step 5: Return to the oven and bake until a thermometer reaches at least 60 degrees celsius (140 degrees F) in the thickest part of the ham.
  6. Step 6: Rest for 10 minutes before serving.
