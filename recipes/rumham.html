Rum Ham

Description

Ham soaked in rum. It is loaded with booze. Eating your drinks? That's genius!

Ingredients

  • Meat: 5 lbs of bone-in fully-cooked ham
  • Mustard: 1⁄3 cup Dijon mustard
  • Sugar: 3⁄4 cup brown sugar
  • Pineapple: 1 can of pineapple slices, undrained
  • Rum fluid ounces of orange-flavored rum
  • Cornstarch: 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • Water: 1⁄4 cup of water
